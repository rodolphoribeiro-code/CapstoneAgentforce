THE DINER — DINING POLICIES GUIDE
Version 2.0 — 2026

This document contains all official dining policies for The Diner restaurant group.
Restaurants: La Bella Italia, Sakura Garden, El Rancho, The Brooklyn Grill, Maison Paris.

---

CANCELLATION POLICY

Reservations may be cancelled free of charge up to 24 hours before the scheduled booking time.
Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance may incur a no-show fee of $15 per guest.
No-shows without any prior cancellation will be charged the full no-show fee.
To cancel, customers can use the Aria assistant, call the restaurant directly, or email the reservation team.
Cancellations for group bookings (8 or more guests) must be made at least 48 hours in advance to avoid charges.

---

GROUP BOOKING POLICY

Groups of 8 or more guests are considered large group reservations.
Large group reservations require a booking at least 48 hours in advance.
A credit card guarantee is required for groups of 10 or more guests.
Group menus may be required for parties of 15 or more — please contact the restaurant directly to arrange.
Split billing is available for groups up to 10 guests. For groups larger than 10, a single payment method is preferred.
Groups of 20 or more require a dedicated event coordinator — contact The Diner events team directly.

---

DRESS CODE POLICY

Smart casual attire is expected at all The Diner restaurants.
Sleeveless tops, flip-flops, and athletic wear are not permitted in the dining room.
Maison Paris has a formal dress code: collared shirts are required for gentlemen; jeans are not permitted.
The Brooklyn Grill and El Rancho have a relaxed smart casual policy.
Children's dress code is relaxed — smart casual is appreciated but not strictly enforced.
Guests who do not meet the dress code may be asked to adjust their attire or may be refused entry.

---

PAYMENT METHODS

All major credit and debit cards are accepted at all locations: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover.
Contactless and mobile payments are accepted at all locations: Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay.
Cash is accepted at The Brooklyn Grill and El Rancho only. La Bella Italia, Sakura Garden, and Maison Paris are cashless.
Gift cards issued by The Diner are accepted at all locations. Gift cards cannot be used for online or phone bookings.
Gratuity of 18% is automatically added for groups of 8 or more.

---

LATE ARRIVAL POLICY

Reservations are held for 15 minutes past the confirmed booking time.
After 15 minutes without contact, the table may be released to walk-in or waitlist customers.
If a guest is running late, they should contact the restaurant or message the Aria assistant to extend the hold.
Arriving more than 30 minutes late without notice is treated as a no-show and the no-show fee applies.

---

CHILDREN POLICY

Children of all ages are welcome at all The Diner restaurants.
High chairs and booster seats are available at all locations — please request when booking.
A dedicated children's menu is available at La Bella Italia, El Rancho, and The Brooklyn Grill.
Sakura Garden and Maison Paris do not offer a dedicated children's menu but can accommodate children's dietary needs with advance notice.
Baby changing facilities are available at La Bella Italia and The Brooklyn Grill.

---

PET POLICY

Pets are not permitted inside any of The Diner restaurants.
Well-behaved dogs on a leash are welcome on outdoor terrace seating areas where available.
El Rancho and The Brooklyn Grill have outdoor seating areas that welcome dogs.
Service animals are always welcome inside all restaurants.
Guests with pet allergies should inform the restaurant when booking.

---

SPECIAL REQUESTS AND DIETARY NEEDS

Special requests including dietary restrictions, allergies, seating preferences, and celebrations should be noted at the time of booking.
All restaurants can accommodate vegetarian and gluten-free requirements with advance notice.
Vegan options are available at all restaurants. Sakura Garden has the most extensive vegan menu.
For guests with severe or life-threatening allergies, please contact the restaurant directly before arriving so the kitchen can take appropriate precautions.
Birthday and anniversary setups including candles, decorations, and customized dessert plates are available with 24-hour advance notice.
Live music and private dining rooms are available at Maison Paris and The Brooklyn Grill — contact events team.

---

RESERVATION MODIFICATION POLICY

Reservations can be modified up to 2 hours before the booking time at no charge.
Party size changes of more than 4 guests require 24 hours notice.
Changing the restaurant location is treated as a cancellation of the original booking and a new booking — the cancellation policy applies.
Date and time changes within the same restaurant can be made up to 2 hours before the original booking time.

---

VIP GUEST PROGRAM

Guests who have completed 5 or more confirmed bookings with The Diner qualify as VIP members.
VIP guests receive: priority seating, complimentary welcome drink, dedicated concierge line.
VIP status is tracked automatically — guests are recognized when they verify their identity through the Aria assistant.
VIP guests with 10 or more bookings receive additional benefits including a complimentary dessert on each visit.
